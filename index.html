Our Story

Founded in 2010, RP Construction Services began as a small team of passionate craftsmen dedicated to quality renovations. What started as bathroom remodels has grown into a full-service construction company serving residential and commercial clients across the region.

We take pride in transforming spaces while maintaining the highest standards of workmanship. Every project receives the same attention to detail, whether it's a simple plumbing fix or a complete kitchen overhaul.
